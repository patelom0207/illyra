What's Illyra??

Find and Track

Illyra is a student-built platform designed to simplify campus life. We help students discover academic, career, and wellness resources through a trusted, curated database. By centralizing campus services into one easy-to-navigate space, we aim to empower students to make the most of their university experience while reducing the confusion of scattered information.

How it Works

  1. Sign Up: Verify your student email to join the community.
  2. Search or Browse: Explore resources and opportunities available on campus.
  3. Get AI-Powered Recommendations: Receive personalized suggestions based on your interests, major, and activity
